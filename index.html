Nomograms

Nomograms are a nice way to visualize numerical relations of parameters in an equation. Printing them to paper and reading values with a ruler is a good way to grasp the relations between the parameter values.

Tools like Processing on the other hand provide an easy way to implement interactive sketches.

So why no put both together and get interactive nomograms?

After researching the mathematical background these are the results I came up with.
