Nomograms

Nomograms are a nice way to visualize numerical relations of parameters in an equation. Printing them to paper and reading values with a ruler is a good way to grasp the relations between the parameter values.

Tools like Processing on the other hand provide an easy way to get the nomograms more interactive.

Websites I found information and inspiration about nomograms:

  • Wikipedia: Nomgram Wikipedia
  • Mathematical background: Nomogram Intro
